Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, website URL.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Paddle — we never store card details.
  • Usage data: Chatbot conversation counts, dashboard activity.
  • Website data: Your website URL for deployment purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deploy and manage your AI chatbot.
  • To process payments and send receipts.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To send service updates and notifications.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Paddle — for payment processing.
  • OpenAI — for AI chatbot functionality (conversation data only).

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your data. Payment data is handled entirely by Paddle's PCI-compliant infrastructure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your subscription is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for session management and analytics. No third-party advertising cookies are used.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion.
  • Opt out of marketing emails.
