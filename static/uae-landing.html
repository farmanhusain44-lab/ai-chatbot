Everything you need to know before getting started.
Does BotifyAI support Arabic? ▼
Yes — Arabic support is built-in and automatic. BotifyAI detects whether a customer writes in Arabic or English and responds in the same language. No configuration needed. This is a core feature designed specifically for the UAE market.
How long does setup take? ▼
On average, 5 minutes. You get a single line of code — paste it into your website and you're done. If you need help, our team installs it for you via WhatsApp within 1 hour, completely free.
Will it work on my website platform? ▼
Yes — BotifyAI works on WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Webflow, custom HTML sites, and any other platform. If your platform supports custom code injection (which almost all do), BotifyAI will work.
Can the bot be trained on my business information? ▼
Yes — on the Professional and Enterprise plans, the bot is trained specifically on your products, services, pricing, FAQs, and policies. It gives accurate, business-specific answers instead of generic responses.
Is there a free trial? ▼
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied within 7 days for any reason, we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.
What happens if the bot can't answer a question? ▼
When the bot reaches the limit of its knowledge, it gracefully tells the customer it will connect them with a team member — and captures their contact details so you can follow up. No lead is ever lost.
