Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2025

✅ 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee — If you are not satisfied with your AI chatbot within 7 days of purchase, we will issue a full refund. No questions asked.

1. Eligibility for Refund

  • Refund request must be made within 7 days of the initial payment.
  • Valid for first-time purchases only.
  • Subsequent monthly renewals are non-refundable after the billing date.

2. How to Request a Refund

Email us at support@aichatbot.ae with:

  • Your name and email used during purchase.
  • Your order/transaction ID.
  • Reason for refund (optional but helpful).

We will process your refund within 3-5 business days.

3. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. Cancellation stops future billing but does not trigger a refund for the current period (unless within the 7-day window).

4. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Requests made after 7 days of purchase.
  • Monthly renewal charges after the first month.
  • Accounts suspended due to Terms of Service violations.

5. Contact

For refund requests or questions: support@aichatbot.ae
