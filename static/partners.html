Frequently Asked

Everything you want to know

Do I need to know coding or setup the bot myself? ▼
No. Our team installs BotifyAI on every client site for free (via WhatsApp support). You just introduce them — we handle 100% of the technical work.
How and when do I get paid? ▼
Commissions are credited to your UPI, bank, or PayPal on the 5th of every month for all payments collected in the previous month. Minimum payout ₹500 (rolls over if below).
What if my client cancels after a month? ▼
You still keep the commission for every month they were paying. Your future commissions stop only when the client cancels — no clawback, ever.
Can I refer clients outside India (UAE, Saudi, etc.)? ▼
Yes. BotifyAI supports 22+ Indian languages plus Arabic and English. UAE plans start at AED 499/mo. Same 20-35% commission applies to international referrals.
Are there any hidden costs or contracts? ▼
Zero. No signup fee, no minimum clients, no exclusivity, no long contracts. Cancel your partnership anytime. You keep commissions on all active referrals even after leaving.
What marketing support do I get? ▼
A complete partner kit: pitch deck (PDF), 60-second demo video, WhatsApp scripts, social media banners, case studies, and a personal WhatsApp channel with our team for any question.
Can I whitelabel BotifyAI as my own product? ▼
Once you reach Elite tier (50+ clients), we offer a whitelabel option — your logo, your domain, your pricing. Talk to us for custom terms.
